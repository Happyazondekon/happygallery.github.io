Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer photography services for events?

Yes, I offer photography services for various events including weddings, corporate events, family gatherings, and more. Please contact me with your specific needs for customized packages.

Can I purchase prints of your photographs?

Absolutely! Most of the photographs in my gallery are available for purchase as prints. You can specify your preferred size and finish. Contact me directly for pricing and availability.

Do you offer photography workshops or classes?

Yes, I periodically offer workshops for photographers of all levels. These workshops cover various aspects of photography from technical skills to artistic expression. Subscribe to my newsletter to stay updated on upcoming workshops.

What areas do you service?

I'm based in Cotonou, Benin, but I'm available for travel both nationally and internationally. Travel fees may apply depending on the location.
